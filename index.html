ABOUT US

Welcome to Mang Inasal, where we bring the flavors of the Philippines to every table. Founded in 2003, Mang Inasal has been serving delicious Filipino food with passion and dedication. Our commitment to quality ingredients and authentic recipes has made us a beloved household name across the country.

At Mang Inasal, we believe in celebrating the rich culinary heritage of the Philippines. From our signature Chicken Inasal to our mouthwatering Halo-Halo, each dish is a celebration of Filipino flavors and culture. With over 500 branches nationwide, we strive to provide a memorable dining experience for our customers, evoking the warmth of Filipino hospitality in every meal.

Come and experience the taste of Mang Inasal today!
